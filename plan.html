Plan and Store

Planning meals is one of the most effective ways to reduce food waste and save on food bills. Whether you prefer to keep things flexible or you love to have everything organised, there are simple steps you can take to make planning meals a bit easier.

What type of meal planner are you?

Meal Planning

Make a weekly or monthly meal plan. Try to choose recipes so that ingredients for one meal can be used in another. Need half a carton of cream on Monday? Use up the other half in a pasta dish the following day. Make a rough meal plan before you shop but leave some wiggle room. Only plan meals for 3 or 4 days, no need to be too ambitious! Try to include food you have already. Use a recipe app to find new ideas.

Shopping List

Keep a running tally of what you need to pick up as it runs out. A blackboard in the kitchen or a shopping list on the fridge is a handy way of doing this. Start with the list of foods you’ve used up and your meal plan will make writing your shopping list quick and easy. Check your fridge and cupboards to see what you do and don’t have. Alternatively, you can take your meal plan with you and use it as your shopping list.
